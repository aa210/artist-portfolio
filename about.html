ABOUT ME

Hey! I’m a Nigerian-American artist and illustrator who was born and raised in the Philadelphia region of the United States. My portfolio consists of colourful, whimsical, witty, yet realistic, portrayals of people, fashion, still life, architecture, and nature. Much of my inspiration comes from vintage fashion illustrations, children’s book illustrations, late 20th century pop culture, classical oil paintings, and my travels abroad. The mediums I use are oil, acrylic, and watercolour paint, graphite and coloured pencils, and ink.

I’m available for collaborations, commissions, creative opportunities, or just to chat. Contact me @ adesiartwork[at]yahoo.com .
